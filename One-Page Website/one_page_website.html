Visit Muscatine

The Many Great Sites of Muscatine

Mississippi River

Muscatine sits right on the bank of the Mississippi River

Weed Park

Weed Park is the largest park in Muscatine.

Muscatine Mall

Muscatine Mall was once a great place to shop. Now it is a great location to mall walk.

National Pearl Button Museum

Muscatine used to be the pearl button manufacturing capital of the world.

Muscatine Soccer Complex

This complex has received awards for having the best soccer complex.

The Merrill Hotel

The Merrill Hotel is the nicest hotel to stay at in Muscatine.

Muscatine Art Center

The Muscatine Art Center is a great place to view art and relax in the Japanese inspired garden.

Contrary Brewing Company

This local brewery has a great atmosphere and is located right next to the Mississippi.
